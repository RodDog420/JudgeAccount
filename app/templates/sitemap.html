About Our Platform

Our Mission

JudgeAccount.com is dedicated to promoting judicial accountability and transparency into the character and conduct of United States judges. We serve as a critical resource for voters, litigants, attorneys, and citizens seeking verified information about judicial officers prior to elections, retention votes, and appointments.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive Judge Database: Search judges by name, court, location, and other criteria
  • Verified Reviews: Read firsthand accounts from individuals who appeared before judges
  • Media Documentation: Access verified news articles and reports about judicial conduct
  • Transparent Moderation: Community-driven reporting with rigorous content standards
  • Voter Resources: Information to support informed decisions in judicial elections

Core Values

We operate with unwavering commitment to transparency, accountability, fairness, respect, integrity, authenticity, justice, courage, and humility. These values guide every aspect of our platform and inform our Community Guidelines.
