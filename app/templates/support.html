Support Our Mission

Help Us Promote Judicial Accountability

JudgeAccount.com is dedicated to transparency and accountability in the American judicial system. Your support helps us maintain this critical resource for voters, litigants, attorneys, and citizens seeking verified information about judicial officers.

As a platform committed to integrity and independence, we rely on support from individuals who believe in the importance of judicial accountability. Every contribution, regardless of size, helps us continue our mission.

Why Your Support Matters

Platform Maintenance

Server hosting, security infrastructure, and technical maintenance require ongoing resources to ensure our platform remains accessible, secure, and reliable.

Content Moderation

Rigorous review of flagged content, verification of media links, and enforcement of Community Guidelines demand dedicated time and expertise.

Development & Innovation

Continuous improvements, new features, enhanced search capabilities, and user experience enhancements help us better serve our community.

Research & Verification

Maintaining accurate judge information, verifying media sources, and ensuring data quality requires ongoing research efforts.

Independence

Community support allows us to remain independent, free from external pressures, and committed solely to transparency and accountability.

Accessibility

Keeping JudgeAccount.com free and accessible to all users ensures that judicial accountability remains a public resource, not a privilege.

How Donations Are Used

We are committed to transparency in how we use donated funds. Your contributions directly support:

  • Infrastructure (40%): Server hosting, cloud storage, bandwidth, security systems, and technical infrastructure
  • Development (30%): Platform improvements, new features, bug fixes, and user experience enhancements
  • Operations (20%): Content moderation, customer support, research, and verification processes
  • Growth (10%): Outreach, education, expanding our judge database, and increasing platform awareness

We maintain rigorous financial accountability and may provide detailed financial reports to major donors upon request.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is protected
Donor information is kept strictly confidential. We do not sell, share, or publicly disclose donor identities without explicit permission. All payment processing is handled through secure, encrypted platforms that comply with industry standards.

Non-Financial Support

If you're unable to contribute financially, there are other valuable ways to support our mission:

  • Share Reviews: If you've appeared before a judge, submit a thoughtful, accurate review
  • Submit Media Links: Help document newsworthy information about judicial conduct
  • Spread the Word: Tell others about JudgeAccount.com, especially before judicial elections
  • Provide Feedback: Help us improve by sharing suggestions and constructive criticism
  • Engage Responsibly: Model our core values of integrity, respect, and truthfulness in your interactions
